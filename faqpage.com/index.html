1. Safehold

What is safehold?

SafeHold is a desktop app for sending, receiving and mining coins on our network (currently the test network). Safehold makes accessing Ellcrys functionalities easy and straightforward;Generating blockchain transactions can be as effortless as accepting them.

How do i set up safehold on my PC?
  1. Download safehold on your computer
  2. Launch and enter your preferred password
  3. A list of phrase will be shown to you.
  4. Click “next” and enter seed phrase shown to you.
  5. An “overview page” will be displayed to you showing your Ellcrys account and address.
